  • Cannot provide verifiable source citations.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data used for training?

No. Your data is strictly isolated and never used to train our base models or shared with other users.

What file formats are supported?

We currently support PDF, TXT, and Markdown. DocX and Notion support is coming in Q1 2026.

Is there a free trial?

Yes! The Free Tier allows you to upload up to 5 documents and chat unlimitedly. No credit card required.

How does RAG work securely?

We chunk your documents locally or in isolated containers, vectorizing them without exposing raw data to public model APIs until inference time.
